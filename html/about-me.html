About Me

Founded in 2025 in Saint Louis, Missouri, R&G Consulting Services LLC began with the vision of a single recruiter who, leveraging extensive market expertise in the USA, Mexico, and LATAM, set out to create a company that bridges talent across borders. Our mission is simple: help companies find the best IT and high-demand talent with agility fast, precise, and hire.
